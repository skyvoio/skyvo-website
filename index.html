Use Cases

Real-world applications across industries

Roof & Facade Inspection

Problem: Manual inspections are slow, dangerous, and often miss critical defects.

Value: Aerial thermography and high-resolution imaging identify leaks, structural issues, and maintenance needs without scaffolding or rope access.

Deliverables: Thermal maps, annotated orthomosaics, defect reports with GPS coordinates.

Construction Progress

Problem: Tracking multi-site progress manually is time-consuming and error-prone.

Value: Automated drone flights capture site conditions weekly, enabling accurate progress tracking and stakeholder reporting.

Deliverables: Time-lapse orthomosaics, volumetric analysis, progress dashboards.

Solar & Wind O&M

Problem: Inspecting renewable energy assets is labor-intensive and disrupts operations.

Value: Thermal and visual drone inspections detect panel defects, turbine blade damage, and hotspots in a fraction of the time.

Deliverables: Thermal anomaly reports, blade condition assessments, maintenance prioritization.

Utilities & Industrial Inspection

Problem: Inspecting power lines, pipelines, and industrial facilities poses safety risks and requires downtime.

Value: Drones and ground robots inspect assets remotely, reducing risk and enabling predictive maintenance.

Deliverables: Condition reports, 3D models, AI-annotated defect maps.

Agriculture & Environmental

Problem: Monitoring large agricultural or environmental sites manually is inefficient and lacks precision.

Value: Multispectral imaging and AI analytics provide crop health insights, erosion monitoring, and habitat assessment.

Deliverables: NDVI maps, change detection reports, environmental impact assessments.

Security & 2D–3D Mapping

Problem: Traditional surveying and security assessments are slow and lack comprehensive spatial context.

Value: High-accuracy mapping and 3D modeling provide detailed site intelligence for security planning and asset management.

Deliverables: GeoTIFF maps, point clouds, CAD/IFC-ready 3D models.

Compliance & Safety

Skyvo operates under Transport Canada RPAS Advanced Operations certification with SFOC (Special Flight Operations Certificate) capabilities as required. Every deployment includes comprehensive risk assessments, flight planning, data governance protocols, and full insurance coverage. Safety and regulatory compliance are non-negotiable foundations of our work.

Frequently Asked Questions

What about airspace permissions and flight approvals?

We handle all airspace coordination, including SFOC applications for controlled airspace. Our Transport Canada Advanced RPAS certification allows us to operate in complex environments, and we conduct thorough site assessments to ensure compliance with all regulations.

What formats do you deliver data in?

We provide industry-standard formats including GeoTIFF for orthomosaics, LAS/LAZ for point clouds, CAD/IFC for BIM handoffs, PDF reports with annotations, and custom dashboards. All deliverables are georeferenced and ready for integration into your existing workflows.

What are typical lead times?

Discovery assessments typically take 1–2 weeks. Pilot projects run 60–90 days. Once deployed, routine inspections can be scheduled with 3–5 business days' notice, weather permitting. Rush services are available for urgent needs.

What's your coverage area?

We're based in the Greater Toronto Area and serve clients across Southern Ontario. For larger projects or multi-site deployments outside this region, we can coordinate with trusted partners or mobilize our team as needed.

Who owns the data?

You do. All data collected during your project is your property. We retain a copy for quality assurance and to support ongoing services, but you have full rights to use, share, and archive the data as you see fit.

What about insurance and liability?

Skyvo carries comprehensive commercial general liability and aviation liability insurance. We provide certificates of insurance upon request and can add clients as additional insured parties as needed.
